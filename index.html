Scan, Search, Learn, and Access Beauty

Access Beauty is an innovative and inclusive technology solution designed to tackle accessibility challenges in the beauty industry. Search or scan the barcode of beauty products to get information on their accessibility features. Our mission is to empower individuals with physical, visual, and cognitive disabilities to make informed purchasing decisions that meet their specific needs.

Search

The search function allows users to easily search for beauty products by name. This feature is designed to cater to the needs of individuals with physical, visual, and cognitive disabilities. It provides information on the physical, visual, and cognitive accessibility features of a product, such as ease of opening, large text, and simplified instructions.

Scan Barcode

The scan barcode feature is another valuable feature of the Access Beauty app. It allows individuals scan the barcode of a beauty product to get information on its accessibility features. This feature also provides information on the physical, visual, and cognitive accessibility features of a product.
